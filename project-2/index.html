Cake Batter Shake!

Made by Caitlyn Patel

About

While experimenting with different shakes and smoothies, I accidentally created the BEST shake ever. With simple ingredients and being easy to make, it's a dessert you must try! I don't know about you, but I love a good dessert. When I realized I could also make a (somewhat) healthier option to this sweet treat, I started to experiment with different ingredients. And ended up with the best recipe for a cake batter shake!

What will you need

  • A blender
  • A cup
  • A box of yellow cake mix
  • A cup of milk of your choice (I suggest whole milk)
  • Non-fat vanilla frozen yogurt
  • Sprinkles and whipped cream if you want them (which I suggest!!)
