The Best Cake Batter Shake!

About

While experimenting with different shakes and smoothies, I accidentally created the BEST shake ever. With simple ingredients and being easy to make, it's a dessert you must try! I don't know about you, but I love a good dessert. When I realized I could modify this sweet treat, I started to experiment with different ingredients, and ended up with not only the best recipe for a cake batter shake, but different alternatives too! Usually with a cake batter shake, you use vanilla ice cream, but I always using a nonfat frozen yogurt. It tastes exactly the same, but is a little less heavy. Also, with this formula, you can make a dairy free alternative, which consists of almond milk, dairy free ice cream of your choice (some use coconut milk icecream), and cake batter mix (most consist of NO dairy).
