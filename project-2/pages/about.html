Lets Start Blending

Get your ingredients together and let's make the best cake batter shake you've ever had!

Instructions

Cake Batter Shake for one
  1. Get out all of your ingredients
  2. Measure 1 cup of milk of your choice
  3. Pour milk into blender
  4. Measure 1 tablespoon of cake batter mix (or 2 if you want it to taste more like cake)
  5. Pour cake batter into blender
  6. Now, take one scoop of icecream and put it in the blender
  7. Mix it up, until everything is blended
  8. Pour into cup and enjoy!
BONUS

Put whipped cream on the top of your shake and add as many sprinkles as you want!
